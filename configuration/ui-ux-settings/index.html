UI/UX Settings

Use the UI/UX settings to customize user experience and modify preferences, options, and configurations to tailor the UX according your needs and preferences.

You can limit user actions and configure notification panel, tiles, enable/disable battery and notification LEDs, show/hide notification dots, status and navigation bars, enable/disable do not disturb mode and physical keyboard shortcuts.

Notification Settings​

Notifications​

Use this section to enable/disable the notification panel on your home screen, hide/show silent conversations and notifications to keep them private on your lock screen, hide/show the manage button under the notifications, hide/show notification of managed networks, enable/disable the long press on app icons to see notifications.

Quick Settings​

Use this section to configure tiles.

LEDs​

Use this section to configure the LEDs behavior.

Restriction policies​

Use these settings to limit user actions and prevent errors, secure data, and ensure platform compliance.

Notification dots​

Show/hide notification dots to signal unread activity or updates without demanding direct user interaction.

Do Not Disturb mode​

Controls how notifications, phone calls, and alerts interrupt you. When enabled, it silences or filters audible sounds, vibrations, and visual pop-ups based on your selected priority rules to help you focus or sleep without distractions.

OptionDescription
OffDisables the restriction entirely. All notifications, calls, and alerts come through normally with full sound and vibration.
Show filteredAllows only pre-approved notifications to break through the silence. This usually includes starred contacts, repeat callers, or critical priority apps.
Show alarmsSilences all standard notifications, messages, and phone calls. It exclusively permits programmed alarms to sound so you do not oversleep.
Show nonelocks all visual and audible alerts completely. The device remains fully silent, turning off sounds, vibrations, and screen wake-ups for everything, including alarms.

Long press on home button​

If enabled, sets the default behavior to automatically launch your voice assistant when you press and hold the physical or virtual home button.

Automatically sync app data​

Enable this setting to let your apps automatically update information (like emails, calendars, and contacts) in the background. Turning it off stops automatic updates, which helps save your battery and mobile data.

Physical keyboard shortcuts​

Enable to allow the physical keyboard to use shortcut combinations for switching apps and navigating the system. Turning it off limits the external keyboard to basic typing, preventing accidental commands or unauthorized system changes.

Emergency SOS​

Turn this setting on to let users quickly press the power button five or more times to call emergency services and share their location. Turning it off disables this rapid-activation shortcut to prevent accidental emergency dials.

Quickly open camera​

Turn this setting on to quickly open the camera by double-pressing the power button, even when the screen is locked. Turning it off disables this shortcut, requiring you to unlock your device to access the camera app.

Captive portal​

Turn this setting on to automatically open a login page when connecting to public Wi-Fi networks in hotels or airports. Turning it off blocks these login pop-ups, which prevents the device from connecting to public networks that require authentication.

OptionDescription
No InternetKeeps your device connected to the Wi-Fi but does not open the login page. You will not have internet access on this network.
User PromptedShows a notification asking you to sign in when a login page is found. Tap the notification to open the page and connect to the internet.
DisconnectedAutomatically disconnects your device from the Wi-Fi network as soon as it detects a login page.

Status bar​

Enables the status bar.

Status bar on lock screen​

Enables the status bar when the screen is locked.

Hide status bar​

Hide the status bar from the top of your screen for a clean, full-screen view.

Hide navigation bar​

Turn this setting on to hide the Home, Back, and Recent Apps buttons at the bottom of your screen to enjoy a larger, full-screen view.

Recent apps​

Turn this setting on to display your recently used applications when you press the Overview button (the square icon at the bottom of the screen). Turning it off blocks the button from opening your app history, keeping your recently opened apps private.

Hide split screen​

Turn this setting on to remove the split-screen option from your recent apps view, preventing apps from being run side-by-side. Turning it off keeps the option available so you can open two apps on the screen at the same time.

Upside down screen rotation​

Turn this setting on to allow your screen to rotate a full 180 degrees when you turn your device completely upside down.

Screenshots​

Turn this setting on to allow taking pictures of your device screen using hardware buttons or gestures. Turning it off blocks screenshots completely to protect private information from being captured or saved.

Clipboard​

Turn this setting on to let you copy and paste text, links, and pictures between different apps. Turning it off blocks the clipboard, preventing copied information from being pasted into other apps to protect your private data.

OptionDescription
NoneCompletely blocks copying and pasting. You cannot copy or paste any information between apps to keep your data fully secure.
Only TextAllows you to copy and paste written words and numbers only. Photos, files, and links cannot be shared through the clipboard.
AllAllows you to copy and paste all types of information freely, including text, links, and pictures between any of your apps.

Hide app info​

Turn this setting on to hide the "App Info" button from your recent apps screen, preventing quick access to deep app settings or force-closing options. Turning it off keeps the button visible so you can easily check app details and manage storage.

Reboot safe mode​

Turn this setting on to allow users to reboot the device into Safe Mode by long-pressing the on-screen power button. Safe Mode starts the device with only original apps running, which helps with troubleshooting. Turning it off disables this shortcut to prevent users from bypassing app restrictions.

Vibration on charge​

Turn this setting on to make your device vibrate briefly when you plug it into a charger, giving you physical confirmation that power is connected.

Restrict access​

Turn this setting on to stop other apps from automatically opening your device's Settings menu. Turning it off allows apps to quickly open the correct settings page when they need you to change a preference.

Screen Cast​

Turn this setting on to let your device share its screen with TVs or external monitors wirelessly.

Quick Share​

Turn this setting on to easily share photos, links, and files with nearby devices without internet. Turning it off stops other devices from seeing your phone or sending files to you.

Chromebook​

Turn this setting on to connect your phone to a Chromebook, letting you text from your computer, share your internet, and unlock your laptop with your phone.

UI restrictions​

Hide search bar​

Turn this setting on to remove the search bar from the top of your Android Settings menu for a cleaner layout.

Dim Airplane mode​

Turn this setting on to lock Airplane Mode completely, preventing users from turning it on or off in the Android settings menu.

Dim WiFi share options​

Turn this setting on to lock the Wi-Fi 'Share' option, preventing users from seeing or sharing network passwords via QR codes.

Dim Do Not Disturb​

Turn this setting on to gray out the Do Not Disturb option in your Android settings menu, preventing users from tapping it or changing their notification rules.

Dim notification settings​

Turn this setting on to lock your notification settings, preventing users from changing how apps send alerts, sounds, or badges.

Dim notification dot​

Turn this setting on to lock the notification dot feature, preventing users from turning app badges on or off over home screen icons.

Dim screensaver options​

Turn this setting on to gray out the screensaver menu, preventing users from changing the screensaver style or timing.

Dim accessibility​

Turn this setting on to gray out the accessibility menu, preventing users from changing tools like screen readers, font sizing, or magnification shortcuts.

Dim long press home button​

Turn this setting on to lock the home button shortcut, preventing users from changing what happens when they press and hold the home button. Turning it off restores access, allowing users to choose whether this gesture launches the digital assistant or stays turned off.

Dim auto sync app​

Turn this setting on to lock the automatic data sync feature, preventing users from turning background updates on or off.

Dim physical keyboard shortcuts​

Turn this setting on to gray out the physical keyboard shortcuts menu, preventing users from changing or adding hardware key combinations.

Dim reset options​

Turn this setting on to lock the device reset menu, preventing users from factory resetting the phone or clearing network settings.
