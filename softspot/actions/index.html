Actions

You can use SoftSpot to quickly switch between the applications you actively use. Tap Configure actions > > Application to add the applications you want to launch with SoftSpot.

Only one action is active at a given moment. Tap the SoftSpot to launch the application. You can switch between actions by swiping left and right on the SoftSpot.

To remove an application from the actions list, tap and swipe right.
